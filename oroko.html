Chemical Resonance invites Moone

With Weareallchemicals, Moone

Lagos

Afro Tech

Afro House

Gqom

With Guest: Moone (They/Them) is an international Dj and writer based in Abidjan, Côte D’Ivoire. Starting out early 2021, the artist has crafted their sound around African Electronic Dance Music. Mixing a wide range of genres including Afrotech, Afro House, Gqom and Kuduro, they curate eclectic, high energy yet very emotional sets that leave a long-lasting mark on the crowds experiencing them.

As a DJ, Moone's intention with the music they share is to make people feel something, always. They have no specific goal other than this one, for the sound to move you. And however the music touches you is directly linked to however you receive it. How open you are and how much you’re willing to surrender to it. There’s no trick and it’s always about the music.
